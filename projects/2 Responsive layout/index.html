Tips to Learn Coding

Starting to Code?

If you’re teaching yourself how to code, you may have more questions than answers when you’re starting out. What do you need to learn? How do you figure out how to fix bugs? And how do you stay motivated when it just seems like an impossible task? If you’re worried about your chances of success in coding, don’t fret. Check out these 10 tips — I hope they will encourage and motivate you!

7 Learning Tips

  1. Have a concrete goal in mind.
  2. Pick a plan and stick to it.
  3. It’s a marathon, not a sprint.
  4. Slow and steady wins the race.
  5. Don’t compare your progress to others.
  6. Make a habit of fitting coding into your daily schedule.
  7. Learn how to research topics and solve problems.
